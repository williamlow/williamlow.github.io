Yangon At Rest

A small collection of photos taken between 1:00 and 3:00 AM in Yangon, Myanmar. I lived in Yangon for several years, and some of my favourite memories are of cycling around the city at night. In the day Yangon's streets as are frantic and exciting as any big South-East Asian city, which makes the calm of night all the more captivating. I took these pictures to help preserve those memories.
